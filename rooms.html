Amenities Rooted in Comfort and Culture

At AHAM, every amenity is thoughtfully curated to ensure your stay is both peaceful and enriching. Whether you're seeking soulful flavours, cozy evenings, or seamless connectivity—AHAM has it all.

01.

Authentic Kerala Breakfast

At Aham Homestay, wake up to the flavors of Kerala with authentic breakfasts like puttu and kadala curry, soft velleppam with coconut milk, Ilayada steamed in Indian bay leaves, and crispy dosa with chutney. Every dish is homemade with love, using fresh local ingredients, offering you a true taste of Kerala’s rich culinary heritage.

02.

Library

Aham Homestay features an aesthetic, cozy library perfect for quiet moments. Curated with a blend of regional literature, travel stories, and classics, it invites guests to unwind and explore. With warm lighting, earthy décor, and a peaceful ambiance, the library offers a serene escape — ideal for book lovers and those seeking mindful relaxation.

03.

Campfire Evenings

At Aham Homestay, enjoy magical evenings around our cozy campfire setup. Surrounded by nature, guests can unwind, share stories, and create memories under the stars. Perfect for families, friends, or couples, the campfire experience adds warmth and charm to your stay, offering relaxation, laughter, and a touch of rustic bliss in a peaceful hillside ambiance.

04.

Pick and Drop.

Aham Homestay offers a reliable 24/7 pick and drop service for guests, ensuring smooth and safe travel at any hour. Whether arriving late, catching an early flight, or exploring nearby spots, our transport facility provides convenience and peace of mind. Enjoy hassle-free transfers with professional drivers and well-maintained vehicles, tailored to make your stay effortless.
