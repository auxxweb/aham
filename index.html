Aham: A Timeless Sanctuary

Aham is an 80-year-old heritage homestay crafted from traditional mud and timber techniques. Nestled in a quiet valley, it offers simple comforts, heritage meals, and immersive village rhythms — a restorative escape where stories, flavours and nature meet. Welcome to slow living and heartfelt hospitality.
